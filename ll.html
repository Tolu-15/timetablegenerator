Frequently Asked Questions

How does the timetable generator help with my study routine?

Our generator uses spaced repetition algorithms to optimize your study sessions for better retention. It schedules difficult subjects during your peak focus times and incorporates regular breaks to prevent burnout. The system also adapts to your progress, automatically adjusting future sessions based on your performance.

Can I sync my timetable across multiple devices?

Yes! Your timetable automatically syncs across all your devices through our cloud service. Whether you're using our web app, iOS, or Android application, your schedule stays up-to-date everywhere. Real-time updates ensure you never miss an important study session.

How does the notification system work?

Our smart notification system reminds you 10 minutes before each scheduled activity. For study sessions, it shows the subject and recommended resources. For breaks, it suggests quick relaxation techniques. You can customize notification preferences for each subject or activity type.

What kind of study resources will I get?

Based on your subjects, we provide curated resources including video lectures, practice problems, quizzes, and recommended reading materials. Our AI analyzes your progress to suggest increasingly challenging materials as you improve, ensuring you're always learning at the optimal level.

Is there a limit to how many subjects I can add?

Our free plan allows up to 5 subjects with basic scheduling. Premium users can add unlimited subjects and access advanced features like exam preparation modes, collaborative scheduling for study groups, and detailed progress analytics.

How does the generator handle last-minute changes?

When you log unexpected events or missed sessions, our system immediately reorganizes your schedule. It prioritizes urgent tasks while maintaining a balanced workload. The algorithm ensures no subject is neglected and redistributes time proportionally based on your upcoming deadlines and exam dates.
